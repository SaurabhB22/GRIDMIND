# GRIDMIND

### Kazakhstan National Power Grid Intelligence System

GRIDMIND is an AI-powered smart grid monitoring and energy intelligence platform designed to improve energy efficiency, grid reliability, and operational decision-making across Kazakhstan's power network.

The platform combines real-time grid monitoring, machine learning-based forecasting, interactive transmission network visualization, and intelligent analytics to provide a comprehensive view of the national power infrastructure.

Developed under the theme **AI for Energy Efficiency and Smart Grids**, GRIDMIND demonstrates how artificial intelligence can support predictive energy management, grid stability, renewable integration, and sustainable power distribution.

# Problem Statement

Modern power grids face increasing challenges due to growing energy demand, industrial expansion, renewable energy integration, and aging infrastructure. Traditional monitoring systems often lack predictive capabilities and intelligent decision support, making it difficult to anticipate demand spikes, identify overload risks, and optimize energy distribution.

GRIDMIND addresses these challenges by providing a centralized AI-driven platform for monitoring, forecasting, and analyzing grid performance in real time.
